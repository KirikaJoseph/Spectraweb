Spectraweb: Redefining Digital Excellence

Spectraweb, an initiative by JHUB Africa, aims to revolutionize the online presence of the JKUAT Department of Computing.

  • The project aims to enhance user experience, accessibility, and content organization.
  • Spectraweb will improve access to information about programs, research, faculty, and departmental resources.
  • A team of industrious JHUB innovators is working on the project to ensure the JKUAT Computing Department website is updated and the content is well organized.

The Project

This Initiative aims to modernize and improve the department's online presence by redesigning and upgrading its official website. The project focuses on enhancing user experience, accessibility, and content organization to better serve the needs of students, faculty, staff, prospective students, and external stakeholders. Click here to see the project. The following are some of the key areas the project is focusing on.

Department's Staff Profiles

Explore detailed profiles of our esteemed faculty and staff. Discover their expertise, research interests, and contributions to the field of computing.

Lecturer's Research Work

Access comprehensive information on the research activities and publications of our lecturers. Stay updated on groundbreaking studies and academic achievements.

Website Interactivity and User Experience

Experience a modern, user-friendly interface designed to enhance navigation and engagement. Our revamped site offers interactive features and intuitive design for a seamless experience.

Department's Resources and News

Stay informed with the latest news, events, and resources from the Department of Computing. Access valuable information and updates on academic and departmental activities.
